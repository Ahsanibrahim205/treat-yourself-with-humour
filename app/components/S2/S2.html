Actions you can take to become less serious

  • Learn to explore and experience the funny side of life by systematically andconsciously introducing more humour into your life. You can do this in a number of ways
    1. Adopt the posture of a positive, happy person, stand upright, breathe and smile.
    2. Find a mirror and smile into it playfully
    3. Practise laughing out loud as a mood-altering discipline
    4. Get into the habit of browsing through the TV / radio guides to find humorous programmes at least once a week. Watch a few!
    5. Screen saver on your PC of something you find humorous.
    6. Same idea with a poster, photograph, cartoon on your wall at work or home.
    7. Occasionally exploring bookshops the section with light –hearted books and even purchasing one sometimes.
  • Any number of the hilarious DVD’s and CD’s can trigger your laughter. Viewing or listening to them will help the process of taking yourself less seriously.
  • Visualisation techniques can help you
    1. Arelax and conjure up potentially funny scenes – imaginary ones
    2. recall situations in your life where something very funny occurred.
    3. Identify and keep those feelings you have when you think about these situations.
  • Notice how your previously over serious approach to life is changed by humour and, as you lighten up, the wellness benefits you gain in terms of taking yourself less seriously.
