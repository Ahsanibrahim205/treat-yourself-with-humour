Treat yourself
with humour

How this technique can improve your life

Positive benefits

  • Will raise your energy levels and help promote increased levels of selfworth and self-motivation
  • Increased ability to lighten up / relax in challenging circumstances
  • Relieve the tension between you and others by signalling okay to be more relaxed
  • Will help you see things in a more positive & optimistic light
  • Helps you, and maybeothers, to improve decision making and creative thinking

Reduction in

  • Being too serious
  • Putting off decisions (maybe being fearful of the consequences)
  • Low motivation
  • Potential to be / be seen as hostile and offensive
  • Leads to be perceived by others as a cynic and breeds in you cynicism
  • A tendency towards pessimism
  • Anxiety levels
  • Irritability
